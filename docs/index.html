Privacy Policy for Theodo Extension

Last updated: 2023-11-07

Introduction

At Theodo, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data we collect. This privacy policy outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and sharing of your data when using our Chrome Extension.

Data Collection

The only data we collect through our Chrome Extension is the content of the websites you visit while using the extension. This data is used solely to provide and improve the functionality of the extension.

Use of Data

The website content collected by our Chrome Extension is used to:

  • Enable the extension's features and functions.
  • Improve the user experience.
  • Ensure the stability and security of the extension.

Data Storage

All collected data is stored securely and is only accessible by authorized Theodo personnel. We do not share your data with any third parties, except as required by law.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we have collected. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at the details provided below.
